Professional Summary

I develop machine-learning methods to understand complex biological systems, with a focus on identifying therapeutic targets in cancer. Methodologically, I work with graph-based models, representation learning (embeddings), and multi-omics integration, with particular attention to robustness under distribution shift, selection-bias mitigation, and rigorous evaluation for translational impact.
